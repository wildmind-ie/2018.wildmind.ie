About WildMind

Wild Mind is a festival of nature, a celebration of our natural environment and all things wild.

Over the course of five days, the sleepy village of Fenit, Co. Kerry, will come alive with a wide range of events and activities each of which aims to inform, inspire and immerse us into our natural environment.
