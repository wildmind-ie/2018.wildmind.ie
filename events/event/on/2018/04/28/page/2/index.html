Secret Life Of Crows

28th April 2018 2:00 pm – 4:00 pm
  • Venue: The Beach Cafe
  • Categories: talk

With Ricky Whelan The Crow family has a mixed reputation in Ireland and crows have went from being feared & respected in ancient times to being shot and poisoned as vermin in modern times. This talk explores the heritage, ecology and intelligence of our seven native species of crow.
